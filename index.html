FILM



We have made award winning films across the US, England, Kenya and France in collaboration with our other InCA partners. Several productions require the assistance of more than one InCA group. Africa's Children, a TV and educational special which launched in 2000, is a collaboration between InCA Nairobi and InCA San Francisco.

InCA Nairobi designed the four-color book and supplied producers, directors and film crews for the shooting, digital post-production was shared between Nairobi and San Francisco, and the final editing, educational versioning and publicity work was done by the American office. This level of international expertise, connectedness and competence is unique in a documentary production company like InCA.

We have also serviced a variety of government agencies and parastatals in Uganda, promoting the country's investment potential to the international community.

MANAGEMENT CONSULTANCY



Since its foundation in 1996, the Management Consultancy department has serviced a wide variety of clients from multinational companies to modestly funded NGOs. Without exception, they have been struck by an approach that discards outdated and parochial management styles, replacing them with the more open, less formal approach that is essential to competition in the global economy. Unlike most management consultancies, we do not see our service simply as an extension of the auditing function: we are first and foremost specialists in human resource development.

Our clients have included Kenya Commercial Bank, KK Security, CFC Life Insurance Company, Citibank, Unilever, BAT, Cadbury (K) Ltd, UK AID, the Canadian International Development Agency and several UN Agencies. In the United States, we have worked for the International Women’s Media Foundation and Encompass.
